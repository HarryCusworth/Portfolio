About Me

I am a passionate and motivated full stack developer and I believe my varied work experience paired with my formal education in philosophy and cognitive science have given me with a unique set of strengths. Through working with hard-to-reach demographics I have a good grasp of design and delivery of projects that revolves around users’ complex needs; my background in informal education means that I am capable of communicating technical things to any audience. I am a self-motivated and enthusiastic worker, comfortable delivering high quality work to a deadline and am a quick learner. I have excellent collaboration and facilitation skills honed through work within a co-op. Now more than ever the world needs developers who truly understand users’ needs and capacities as well as how to code.

Outside of coding I like to go on long bike-based adventures, resurrect old mountain bikes, play Magic the Gathering. I have a particular interest in the role of play in intelligence and wrote my PhD thesis on the role of language in certain types of thinking.
